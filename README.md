#  How to Train Your Dragon 2  
**2014 ‧ Animation / Action / Adventure**

---

##  Overview
**How to Train Your Dragon 2** is the epic sequel to *How to Train Your Dragon (2010)*,  
produced by **DreamWorks Animation**. It continues the story of **Hiccup** and his loyal dragon **Toothless**,  
five years after uniting Vikings and dragons on the island of Berk.

 *“The world may be big, but you can make it better… one friendship at a time.”*  

---

##  Plot Summary
> When Hiccup discovers a secret ice cave filled with wild dragons and meets his long-lost mother,  
> he finds himself in a battle to protect peace from a powerful dragon hunter named **Drago Bludvist**.

---

##  Main Characters
1. **Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III** – The brave dragon rider and future chief of Berk.  
2. **Toothless** – Hiccup’s loyal Night Fury and best friend.  
3. **Astrid Hofferson** – Hiccup’s fierce and loyal partner.  
4. **Valka** – Hiccup’s long-lost mother who protects dragons.  
5. **Drago Bludvist** – The ruthless villain who seeks control over all dragons.

---

##  Highlights
- Stunning **animation and flight scenes**  
- Powerful **emotional moments**  
- Memorable **soundtrack** by *John Powell*  
- Themes of **leadership, family, and friendship**
